What is eli5?

ELI5 is short for “Explain Like I’m 5,” a request for a simple explanation to an otherwise complicated question or concept.

How can I answer question?

Just hit EIL5 button follow the basic rule:

  • State it: State the idea clearly, in a few sentences.
  • Elaborate: Explain the idea further in your own words. Elaborate on the concept in a paragraph or less.
  • Exemplify: Provide concrete examples (and counter-examples, if necessary).
  • Summarise: Summarise your explanation.
